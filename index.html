Ashutosh Jha,
Ph.D.

Assistant Professor (Grade-I)  ·  Information Technology & Systems

I study how information and communication technologies (ICTs) diffuse across various societal systems — and what that means for socioeconmic development, public policy, and business. My work sits at the intersection of Information Systems, Techno-Economics, and Public Policy, with a particular focus on New Age Digital Infrastructures, Mobile Communication Technlogies (4G/5G/6G), and Emerging Economies. Before academia, I have also spent several years in the industry working with IBM and Tech Mahindra. I enjoy working with students who ask hard questions and are willing to engage with ambiguity.
